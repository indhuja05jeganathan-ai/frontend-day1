Tirunelveli District

Tirunelveli is a historic city in southern Tamil Nadu, situated on the banks of the Thamirabarani River and popularly known as Nellai. It is one of the oldest cities, known for Nellaiappar Temple, Shaivite traditions, classical architecture, temple festivals, music, and folk arts

Tourism in Tirunelveli

Tirunelveli is a beautiful district in Tamil Nadu famous for Courtallam waterfalls, Agasthiyar falls, Papanasam dam, Manimuthar dam and Nellaiappar Temple.

Heritage & Culture

Tirunelveli has a rich cultural heritage with traditional food like Halwa, Sodhi Kuzhambu and unique temples with Dravidian architecture. The Tamirabarani river plays an important role in agriculture and daily life.

About Paddy Cultivation

Tirunelveli district is well known for its vast and fertile paddy fields. Paddy cultivation is the main agricultural activity in this region.
